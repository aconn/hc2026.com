Frequently Asked Questions

The graduation ceremony is scheduled to begin at 6:00 PM on May 20, 2026. We recommend arriving early to find parking and seating. (Note: Date and time may change - check back for updates.)
Guest limits will be announced closer to the event date. Check back here or on the school website for updates on seating capacity and ticket requirements.
Students should wear dress clothes under their gowns. For boys: dress pants, collared shirt, and dress shoes. For girls: a dress or dress pants with a nice top, and appropriate dress shoes. Avoid overly casual attire like jeans or sneakers.
We are exploring options for live-streaming the ceremony for family members who cannot attend in person. Details will be announced as they become available.
